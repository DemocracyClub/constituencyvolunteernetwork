What now?

Apart from helping with available tasks, before we can really start making a difference we need to try to get at least one volunteer in every constituency in the United Kingdom.

Please think if you can invite some friends who might be interested, or consider volunteering to help in other places (e.g. somewhere you're studying, or somewhere else nearby)

In time, we'll begin to send you occasional emails, asking if you can help with certain tasks. The first major task will be gathering information about candidates who are standing in the next election in your constituency. Read about our purpose and what we're planning.
